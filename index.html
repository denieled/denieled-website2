About

One Identity.

Denieled is a Miami-based live audiovisual production company. We design, program, and operate lighting and LED video wall systems for concerts, shows, festivals, and events — integrating light, visuals, and music into a single cohesive experience.

Not purely a studio. Not purely an artist. A single creative identity expressed across live production, technical execution, and sound. From GrandMA programming to real-time VJ operation, we handle the full visual layer of your production.

Based in Miami. Available nationally.
